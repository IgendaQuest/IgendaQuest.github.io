Participating in the Deering Estate Volunteer Workdays encourages individuals, companies, civic groups, and neighborhood associations to volunteer their time and resources to help maintain the beauty of this historical treasure.

You may clean up the coastline and mangroves areas, our natural areas trails, fulfill community hours, or complete a scouting badge, just to name a few.

Your volunteer efforts will bring smiles to many faces and our community will be a better place as a direct result of your efforts.

CA Miami Family Day

August 14th from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm

The Institute of Contemporary Art, Miami is hosting a Family Day in the Design District's Palm Court on Sunday August 14th, 2016.

We need your assistance to help the event run smoothly. This opportunity is ideal for high school students looking for community service hours in a fun, creative environment! Volunteers will be needed to assist with set up and clean up, to assist children with hands on art making activities, and ushering.

This opportunity is open to volunteers of all ages; encouraged for high school students seeking community service hours.

ICA Miami will offer parking reimbursement, and lunch is on us!

Interested volunteers should respond to this posting to Riana Casas at volunteers@icamiami.org with a first and last name, cell phone number, and mode of transportation.
