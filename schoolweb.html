What is IgendaQuest?

Students oftentimes find themselves losing track of their responsibilities due to a lack of an effective medium where they can keep their school-related activities and assignments ordered. IgendaQuest seeks to make life easier for already stressed-out teens because we believe that organizing, connecting, and planning are essential to a successful academic career. IgendaQuest would like to take the form of a mobile application in order to be accessed on the go by our target audience are high school students and parents. However, we will be launching a website first as a prelude to our ultimate goal. This website will contain features such as 'keep informed' and 'stay organized' in order to create a safe haven for students' whirlwind of to-do lists and endless list of activities. In other words, IgendaQuest is the solution to students' organizational problems!
